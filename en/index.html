Professional presentations, charismatic speakers, interesting topics for discussion — all of these await you at the conference.

Pre-party


Soft skills development
Kyiv, 15a Leiptsyzka str. the office of the company Codemotion

The pre-party will happen on May 17 2019 at 6:00 PM, in the office of the company Codemotion, 1, Kyiv, 15a Leiptsyzka str.. Our speakers will talk about a very important topic for developers, team leads, and CTOs - soft skills’ development. Attendees will be able to listen to our speakers, as well as discuss the issue during the breaks.
